QULTURE Email Templates

Setup Instructions:
  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account
  2. Create a new service (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  3. Create email templates using the templates below
  4. Update the service ID, template ID, and public key in email-service.js
